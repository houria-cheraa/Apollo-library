Book Summary

In Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone, an orphaned boy, Harry Potter, discovers that he is a wizard on his 11th birthday. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes friends and learns about magic. Harry uncovers a secret about a powerful artifact, the Sorcerer's Stone, and with the help of his friends, Ron and Hermione, he prevents the dark wizard, Voldemort, from obtaining it and regaining his strength.
